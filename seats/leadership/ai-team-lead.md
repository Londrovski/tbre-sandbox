### Academic projects feeding the team (recurring, every year)
Bath integrated-Master's (MEng) students do two big projects, second semester each year:
- **Year 3, Sem 2 — GBDP (Group Business Design Project):** a team project that produces a commercial/business deliverable.
- **Year 4, Sem 2 — research project:** an individual technical research project.

Each year a fresh GBDP group comes through. A Team Lead duty is to **set up and oversee the GBDP group**, and to **help integrate any relevant final-year (Y4) research projects into the team's scope of work** — so academic effort lands on real TBRe problems instead of running parallel.

**Reuse workflow (avoid duplicate work):**
- This year's GBDP produced a **commercial report**. That report is intended to feed the **Formula Student business report** that has to be submitted as part of the FS submission — reuse it rather than writing the business report from scratch.
- General principle: when scoping a GBDP brief or a Y4 project, aim it at a deliverable the team needs anyway (a competition doc, a subsystem, a piece of analysis) so one effort satisfies both the academic mark and the team requirement.
